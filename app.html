The six vitals
Cash Position
25%
cash ÷ monthly operating expense
Weighted heaviest because running out of cash — not unprofitability — is what actually kills most SMEs.
Working Capital Runway
20%
(cash + receivables − payables) ÷ burn
The forward-looking half of liquidity — money about to move in or out that a cash snapshot alone would miss.
Receivables Aging
15%
% of receivables overdue 90+ days
Isolates money you're owed but genuinely at risk of not collecting, not just outstanding invoices.
Payables Due
15%
payables due in 30 days ÷ cash
Tests whether your liquid cash actually covers what's about to come due.
Net Margin
15%
net profit ÷ revenue
Profitability matters, but it's a lagging indicator — a business survives low margin longer than it survives no cash.
GST/ITC Leakage
10%
unclaimed ITC ÷ monthly GST payable
A real cash leak worth surfacing — weighted lowest because it's a compliance issue, not a solvency risk.
Where the benchmarks come from
01Liquidity thresholds — drawn from the RBI's Tandon Committee framework, the reference standard Indian banks have used to assess working-capital lending since 1975, plus cash-reserve conventions widely used by CFOs and advisors.
02Profitability benchmarks — published net-margin ranges by industry, cross-checked against India Inc-wide averages.
03Trade-credit norms — the receivables and payables thresholds reflect standard practice for invoice aging and near-term liquidity coverage.
04GST/ITC leakage — no published benchmark exists yet for this one. We use a reasoned placeholder until real data gives us a better one. We'd rather tell you that than hide it.
